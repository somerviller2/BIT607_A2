Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter a name or contact information, we'll be unable to book them in for a service.

We keep your information safe by Storing it in encrypted files and restricting who can access these files.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.

If you'd like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at hello@snazzypaws.co.nz, or 000 000, or 44 Vercoe Road, Beerescourt, Hamilton.
